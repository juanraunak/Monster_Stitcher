# The Life and Character of David

- **David's Anointing**: David was chosen as king while still a shepherd boy, marking the beginning of his divine journey.
- **David and Goliath**: His defeat of Goliath as a teenager highlighted his courage and faith.
- **Saul's Pursuit**: Despite being hunted by King Saul, David showed integrity and restraint.
- **Bathsheba Affair**: David's moral failure with Bathsheba and Uriah was a pivotal moment in his story.
- **Friendship with Jonathan**: Jonathan and David shared a loyal and protective friendship.
- **Kindness to Mephibosheth**: David honored his promise to Jonathan by showing kindness to Mephibosheth.
